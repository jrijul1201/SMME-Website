Fluid Mechanics & Thermal Science

Members of the Fluid Mechanics & Thermal Science group undertake their teaching and research activities in several traditional as well as modern areas. Some key focus areas of the group are multiphase flows, interfacial flows, combustion & radiation heat transfer, granular flows, turbulence, computational fluid dynamics, alternate fuels & IC engines, thermal energy systems, sprays & atomization. Novel numerical techniques and experimental methodologies are routinely employed to assist the investigation.
