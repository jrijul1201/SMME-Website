Program Overview

B. Tech program in School of Engineering had started in 2009 with Mechanical Engineering branch with an intake of 40 students per batch. Currently, the intake in the Mechanical Engineering is 40. School of Engineering has started Civil Engineering program in 2015 with an intake of 20 students. Currently, there are 30 students /per year in civil Engineering. At present, there is total number of 126 students in Mechanical Engineering and 70 students in Civil Engineering pursuing their bachelor degree. In total, 126 students had graduated in Mechanical Engineering till 2018 from the inception of the program.Currently, the School offers two B.Tech. programmes:

  • B.Tech. in Civil Engineering (CE)
  • B.Tech. in Mechanical Engineering (ME)
