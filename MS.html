Program Overview

School of Engineering offering Master of Science (M.S.) by Research in all research areas of Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Materials Engineering. Master of Science (M.S.) by Research program at IIT Mandi is designed to help scholars acquire in-depth knowledge by carrying out advanced research in a chosen area. As compared to M.Tech.program, the M.S. is research oriented and demands a thesis that is a significant contribution to the area of research. In addition to the thesis, students have to complete five courses related to their area of research
