School of Mechanical & Materials Engineering offering Master of Technology (by Research) in all research areas of Mechanical Engineering and Materials Engineering. Master of Technology (by Research) program at IIT Mandi is designed to help scholars acquire in-depth knowledge by carrying out advanced research in a chosen area. As compared to M. Tech. program, the M. Tech (by Research) .It is research oriented and demands a thesis that is a significant contribution to the area of research. In addition to the thesis, students have to complete five courses related to their area of research
