The School of Mechanical and Materials Engineering offers Master of Technology (by Research) or MTech (by Research) program in all research areas of mechanical as well as materials engineering. The MTech (by Research) program at IIT Mandi is designed to help scholars acquire in-depth knowledge by requiring them to undertake research work in a chosen area. In contrast to the MTech program, the MTech (by Research) program is research oriented. Students in this program are expected to submit and defend a thesis.
