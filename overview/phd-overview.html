School of Mechanical & Materials Engineering offering PhD program in all research areas of Mechanical Engineering & Materials Engineering. The Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) is the highest academic degree awarded by IIT Mandi. In this research-based program, the student submits a thesis which is a significant contribution to the research area. The student is expected to publish in top-quality journals and conferences. The program also has a course-work requirement followed by a comprehensive exam. The School offers 6 research area under PhD programs.
